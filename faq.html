Frequently Asked Questions

We realize our large catalog can be quite daunting which is why we offer consultation if you would like our professional opinion. Reach out to us and we will send you a questionnaire to fill out asking about your tastes and home decor needs. After you fill it out, we will take no more than 1 business day to reply with a custom report of recommendations!

All of our wall art comes ready to hang, with hanging hardware affixed to the back and a small red bag of the necessary hanging accessories (a nail, a pair of gloves, and a level). Hanging is fast and easy. There is no need to call in a professional!

If your tracking number shows your package as delivered, and it isn't in your hands, please contact us at customerservice@vestarts.com!

If your wall art has any flaws at all, please do not hesitate to contact us at customerservice@vestarts.com. Please send a picture of the flaw along with your message and we'll get right to work!

If your wall art is from our 'premade' collection, you will have 30 days to return it for a full refund. Reach out to our customer service team for a return label and package your art carefully. Custom Orders are not returnable, but we will work with you to create another that will certainly fulfill your vision at no extra cost.

Each of our prints is constructed in the USA, right in our workshop, by workers paid a fair, living wage.
